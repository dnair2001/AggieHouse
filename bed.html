Apply For A Bed.

Join our Aggie House community as a resident by submitting an application where we can assess how Aggie House can best provide for you. If you need a paper copy of our application, please email us at aggiehousedavis@gmail.com.


Program Details

Get a free bed, today.

Aggie House provides students with a space to stay for 11 weeks per cycle with an option to renew the cycle. All residents are guaranteed a 1 year stay at Aggie House to provide sufficient time to find permanent housing and a network of resources.

Aggie House will provide not only shelter, but also breakfast and dinner, case management services to find permanent and stable housing, and a wonderful community of friendly volunteers. If you have any questions about eligibility or the application process, please email us.
