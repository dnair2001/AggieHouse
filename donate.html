Donate Today.

You can make a difference right now. 100% of donations will go towards housing, food, and other necessities to keep this shelter running.

Donation Details

Make a difference.

Our funds are used to help provide a safe, sustainable, and welcoming space for our students to thrive while they stay at Aggie House. With donor funds, we can increase the ability of Aggie House to serve these students by:

  • Purchasing more nutritious food to empower then through a long day of academic studies and work
  • Purchasing more cleaning supplies to ensure the safety and hygiene of all our residents and volunteers
  • Ensure Aggie House can continue to serve students for years to come

Donor FAQs

How does my donation make a difference?

$5 will feed one student for an entire day

$10 will buy a set of disinfecting wipes

$20 will buy a warm bed comforter

$40 will feed all our residents for a day

Can I make a physical donation?

Yes! If you would like to make a physical donation, such as food or furniture, please contact us via email to discuss more details.

Please contact us if you have any questions.
